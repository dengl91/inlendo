Platform referral program rules

How the referral program works:

The platform user has the opportunity to receive additional income from the referral program, we will take into account all referrals (users invited by you) who came through your referral link.

The user who accepts the Offer to conclude a referral program agreement receives a unique reusable link - an individual referral code that contains information about the Referral account. One referral link is valid for an unlimited number of Referrals and transactions.

The procedure for calculating remuneration:

You will receive 0.5% of the amount of all loan transactions made by the referral-investor within 6 months and 0.5% of the amount of all attracted by the referral-borrower within 3 months.

Funds are credited to the user's account. Referral income is not paid for the fact of registration or the fact of crediting funds to a nominal account.

You receive income only from completed loan transactions.

If you are a financial blogger, the owner of a financial (other specialized) website, if you have questions or suggestions for cooperation, please contact us at support@inlendo.com to discuss the terms of cooperation.

Payment procedure:

The platform transfers your remuneration once a month for the previous accounting month, provided that the amount of the accrued Referral remuneration is at least 100 euros. If the Referral is an individual, then the remuneration is paid minus the personal income tax withheld.
