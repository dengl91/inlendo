Why Inlendo?.

Inlendo is a new generation European crowdlending platform - fully licensed and regulated, with most contemporary software on the market, integrations with market leaders in our field and secure cash flow. We bring together European companies and European investors for direct deal settlements, maximising information flow and profit level for both and decreasing the bureaucracy, document flow and risks - altogether resulting in a much higher time efficiency in their operations for both Borrowers and Investors.

Project evaluation

The projects displayed on the platform have gone through our professional supervision and verification of variety of details, including financial performance of the company and all the aspects of the trade project. We evaluate the previous experience of company and management, we carefully look into supply and sales contracts, we take into consideration factors like reputation of company, management and beneficiaries, we check the financial abilities of borrower for repayments and his actions on deversification of risks. As a result of our evaluation, we provide our own Score to every application on the Platform - exposing the possible risk level for the Investors.

See our internal Inlendo credit policy for SMEs.
